#### Talisman of the Sphere

*Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement)*

When you make an Intelligence (Arcana) check to control a [*sphere of annihilation*](dnd-5e-srd-item-sphere-of-annihilation) while you are holding this talisman, you double your proficiency bonus on the check.
